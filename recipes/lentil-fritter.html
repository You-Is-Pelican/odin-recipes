Lentil Fritters aka Carrot Pies

Nice light and simple, these red lentil fritters never disappoint!

A great source of plant protein,
this recipe can be made up in a pinch,
with the mix happy to be left in the fridge for when you want your next fritter fix!
Feel free to flavour them to your taste!
Once you have the technique down this mixture will become a mainstay in your fridge!

Ingredients

  • 500g New Potatos
  • 150g of Tofu
  • One Brown Onion
  • ¼ Cup of Dill - Chopped
  • ½ tsp of Sea Salt

    • Dressing

  • ¼ Cup of Olive Oil - EXTRA Virgin
  • 2 Tbsp Lemon Juice
  • Zest of One Lemon
  • One Garlice Clove - Minced
  • 2 Tbsp of Dijon Mustard
  • ½ tsp of Sea Salt
  • Black Pepper - To Taste

Steps

  1. Bisect the potatoes - leave the skin on
  2. Submerge in water and add the salt. Boil for 15 minutes or until soft
  3. Meanwhile roast/ airfry the tofu and onion adding oil when neccesary.
  4. Add all dressing ingredients into a bowl and whisk.
  5. Once the potato, tofu and oniion are cooked, combine with the dressing.
  6. Mix in the dill.
  7. Tell no one.
