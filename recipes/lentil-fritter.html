Lentil Fritters aka Carrot Pies

Nice light and simple, these red lentil fritters never disappoint!

A great source of plant protein,
this recipe can be made up in a pinch,
with the mix happy to be left in the fridge for when you want your next fritter fix!
Feel free to flavour them to your taste!
Once you have the technique down this mixture will become a mainstay in your fridge!

Ingredients

  • 150g red lentils
  • 4 large carrots - peeled and grated
  • 1 red onion - peeled and finely sliced
  • 1 large clove of garlic - peeled and chopped
  • 50g gram (chickpea) flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 tablespoon coconut oil, plus more for frying
  • Generous pinch of salt

Steps

  1. Place the red lentils into a pan of boiling water over a medium heat and cook until soft, about 25 minutes.
    Drain and place to one side to cool.
  2. Once cool, combine all the ingredients in a large bowl and mix well, adding a little dash of water if the mixture feels too dry.
    Leave the mixture to one side for 5 minutes.
  3. Scoop out large spoonfuls of the mixture and form them into patty shapes using your hands.
    The mixture should make around 12 fritters.
  4. Heat some coconut oil in a frying pan. Once really hot, place 3-4 fritters in the pan and cook for 5-10 minutes on each side.
    They should be golden on the outside and cooked through.
  5. Tell only those who won't listen.
